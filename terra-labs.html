// 06 — THE CREW

Marine biologists.
Engineers. Captains.
Builders of instruments.

Terra Laboratories was founded by a marine biologist, data scientist, and USCG 100-ton master captain. The practice exists because the people who understand environmental science do not always have time to build the pipelines, and the people who build the pipelines do not always understand the science.

We sit in that gap — on the boat, in the lab, at the keyboard, and increasingly with our heads pointed at the sky. We hire for people who can read a tide table, write a query, and ship a sensor that lasts the season.
