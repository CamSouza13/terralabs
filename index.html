// OBSERVATORY PLATE · ENTRY 001

If Earth were an
exoplanet, what would
we want to know about it?

Imagine our species first reached this rock tomorrow. We would build the best instruments we could to study it. Sensors in the water, networks across the air, satellites watching the surface, databases knitting it all together. We would treat every reef, river, and ridge as a frontier.

We already live here, so we don't. Terra Laboratories thinks that's the mistake. The tools we build for Earth today are the same tools humanity needs at every world we visit next.

// 04 — THE STACK

From sensor
to signal.

4 stages · 1 pipeline
// open at every layer
→ 01 · SENSE

In-situ capture.

Buoy nodes, DriftCams, and the FieldBook app collect environmental data wherever the work is, however unforgiving the conditions.

→ 02 · INGEST

Stream and store.

TerraDB ingests sensor streams, field uploads, and satellite layers into one queryable warehouse. Open at every layer.

→ 03 · ANALYZE

Model and detect.

Built-in models, anomaly detection, and a full notebook environment for your own analyses on top of your own data.

→ 04 · SHIP

Reports and decisions.

Compliance reports, monitoring summaries, and stakeholder dashboards. Generated automatically. Edited by hand.
